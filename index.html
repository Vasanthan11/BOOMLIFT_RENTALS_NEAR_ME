Equipment

Different access options for different kinds of sites

Boom lifts, scissor lifts and man lifts each solve slightly different challenges. These quick bands give you a feel of where each type fits.

Outreach & movement

Boom lifts for complex access points

When your work area is not directly below the machine, boom lifts help you move outwards and over obstacles. They are useful for signage, façade panels, lighting poles and plant structures.

Safe baskets, powered movement and rotation make it easier for your team to reach different points without constantly rearranging equipment.

Click here to check boom lift options near you
Stable vertical access

Scissor lifts for straight-up work

If your job is mainly vertical – such as working on ceilings, services or racking – scissor lifts provide a broader platform and straightforward up-and-down motion.

They are commonly used in warehouses, commercial interiors and plant maintenance where floor condition allows smooth movement.

Compact & focused

Man lifts and AWP units for tighter spaces

Some jobs happen in narrow corridors, between machinery or in smaller rooms. Compact man lifts or aerial work platforms are designed for these tighter spots.

They help you complete focused maintenance without needing large clearances or major rearrangement of existing equipment.
